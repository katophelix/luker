Jimmy

Jimmy signed with Jana as a client in 2002. For 10 years he was Jana's assistant and intern in the office. Answering phones before emails were a thing and stuffing casting envelopes with headshots before online submissions were the norm. He then became an agent with a different agency for 5 years while still remaining a client of Jana. Now 18 years later things have gone full circle as Jimmy now works with Amy as her office manager.

Submissions

If you would like to be considered for representation, please submit a headshot, resume, and reel (if applicable) via mail or email to the appropriate office.

*We DON’T accept walk-ins, please do not come in and drop off your materials in person. *Please DON’T call our offices to follow up on the status of your submission. All submissions are reviewed by our team and we will contact you if we are interested in setting up a meeting for potential representation.
